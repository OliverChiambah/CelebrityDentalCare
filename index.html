About Us


Celebrity Dental Care is a branch of our burgeoning conglomerate, poised to encompass diverse sectors including medicine, fashion, agriculture, and electronics. Specializing in both general and cosmetic dentistry, Celebrity Dental Care recognizes the profound impact of oral health on overall well-being. Our mission is twofold: to provide comprehensive education on oral hygiene to the public and to alleviate dental discomfort while enhancing smiles, thus advancing the standard of dental care in Cameroon. Our confidence in achieving this mission stems from the expertise of our team. Dr. Nyingchuo Bertrand, with seven years of rigorous training in dentistry, brings invaluable experience to our practice. Moreover, Mr. Ngong Oliver contributes a wealth of international expertise spanning program management, software development, and research.
